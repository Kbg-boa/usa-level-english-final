🚀 CE QUI MANQUE POUR PASSER AU NIVEAU SUPÉRIEUR
1️⃣ Il manque un “Tense Power Section”

Tu montres les formes.

Mais il faut montrer quand utiliser chaque temps.

Ajoute :

🎯 When to Use

Past (went)
→ Finished action in the past
“I went yesterday.”

Present Perfect (have gone)
→ Experience / connection to now
“I’ve gone there before.”

Ça développe instinct.

2️⃣ Il manque “Speed Drill Mode”

Exercice ultra rapide :

Complete in 3 seconds:

Yesterday I ____ (go) → went
I have ____ (go) → gone
She ____ (go) every day → goes

Ça crée automatisme.

3️⃣ Il manque “Speaking Drill”

Après écriture :

🎙 Say naturally:
“I’ve gone through the contract.”

Accent + verb ensemble.

4️⃣ Il manque “Phrasal Expansion”

Avec GO, ajoute :

go out
go over
go through
go ahead
go back

Exemple :

“We need to go over the contract.”
Business gold.

5️⃣ Il manque “Pattern Recognition”

Mini challenge :

Which sounds natural?

A) I have went
B) I have gone

Timer 5 seconds.

Ça renforce correction instinctive.
