🎤 Why Speaking Practice = FASTEST Way to Fluency
The Problem: You can read and write, but when you need to SPEAK, your brain freezes!
The Solution: Practice real conversations OUT LOUD. Your brain needs to build the neural pathways for spontaneous speech.
How It Works: Talk to the AI like a real person. It listens, responds naturally, and you build confidence through repetition.
💡 Pro Tip: Don't worry about mistakes! Natives make mistakes too. Focus on communicating your meaning, not perfect grammar!
